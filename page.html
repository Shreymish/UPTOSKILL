Faculty Development Program

Purpose of the Faculty Development Program

A Faculty Development Program (FDP) is designed to foster personal and professional growth among faculty members while positively impacting student, national, and economic development. Key objectives include:

  • Enhancing teaching and research skills
  • Encouraging continuous learning and self-improvement
  • Improving student engagement and learning outcomes
  • Contributing to national knowledge and innovation
  • Strengthening the workforce with skilled graduates
  • Driving economic growth
